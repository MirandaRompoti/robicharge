About Us

Welcome to RobiCharge

At RobiCharge, we provide the ultimate charging solution for your mobile devices. Our innovative charging station offers a convenient and hassle-free way to power up your devices while on the go. With a wide range of portable chargers available for rent, you can easily stay connected throughout your busy day.

Our contactless payment system makes the rental process seamless. Simply tap your smartphone or contactless payment method on our station, select the charger of your choice, and you're good to go. No more searching for outlets or carrying bulky chargers.

Your safety and satisfaction are our top priorities. We maintain our chargers to the highest standards, ensuring that you receive a fully charged and reliable device every time you rent from us.

Experience the freedom of on-the-go charging with RobiCharge. Never worry about running out of battery again. Charge with us and stay powered up!

Venue FAQ's
There is no cost in getting RobiCharge for your Venue. The only thing you need to do is to contact us. We will take care of the rest!
The reason the charging station and the power banks are free to eligible venues is that the end user pays a small fee to use our service. That is how we generate some of our revenue. The only cost for venues is the cost of electricity which can be as little as 1 euro per month.
The venue has no responsibility other than plugging the machine. We take care of the rest!
Customers just tap their payment card on the built-in card reader and a power bank will appear from within the station. Once they've finished charging, customers just return the power bank to the station by placing it in an empty slot. If your customers need help, they can follow the on-screen instructions for assistance.
Customer FAQ's
Rentals start from as little as 2 euros per hour at some locations. After returning the power bank, the rental period ends and the user is charged accordingly. For example, if the rental price at a station was 2 euros per hour and the user's rental period was 2 hours, they would be charged a total of £4 upon returning the power bank. Alternatively, if a rental was 3 euros per hour and the rental period lasted 3 hours, the user would be charged £9 in total. he per hour price can be found on the first screen that appears after tapping on the station.
